CVE stands for Common Vulnerabilities and Exposures. It can be thought of as a fancy term for exploit/vulnerability.
Malware is malicous software. Even though malware and virus are used interchangeably they aren't the same.
Virus is a specific type of malware that infects files as a means to sbread. 
Worm is a specific type of malware that self replicates and spread automatically over the internet. It doesn't even need user interaction to inefect a machine.
Trojan a program desinged to look legit but steals user information and/or opens up pathways for more malware to infect device.
Ransomware is malware the encrypts files and demands payment to unlock. Often files are also deleted over a set interval (one file every 5 minutes).
Rootkit inbeds itself into the boot process of a device giving privileged access to attackers. Advanced root kits can even infect the BIOS/UEFI instead of the OS.
